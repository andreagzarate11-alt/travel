14-day suggested itinerary

The Maldives

Malé · North Malé Atoll · Baa Atoll · Lhaviyani Atoll

Days 1–2

Arrival & Malé City

Arrive Velana International Airport, private speedboat transfer. Explore Malé's colourful Friday Mosque and fish market. Sunset dhoni cruise with Maldivian mezze and chilled Champagne.

Waldorf Astoria Maldives, Ithaafushi

Days 3–5

Overwater villa & house reef

Settle into your overwater villa with glass floor panels and direct lagoon access. Dawn snorkel on the pristine house reef, private sandbank picnic lunch, PADI dives at world-class drop-offs.

Gili Lankanfushi, North Malé Atoll

Days 6–8

Baa Atoll UNESCO Biosphere

Seaplane transfer to Baa Atoll. Snorkel Hanifaru Bay during manta and whale shark season. Underwater dining at Ithaa, spa flotation, bioluminescent night paddle.

Four Seasons Resort Landaa Giraavaru

Days 9–11

Remote island hopping

Private yacht charter across the atolls, stopping at uninhabited islands. Freediving lesson, Maldivian cooking class on deck, stargazing with an onboard astronomer.

Soneva Jani, Noonu Atoll

Days 12–14

Lhaviyani Atoll & farewell

Dolphin sunrise cruise, final reef dive, couples massage in an overwater spa pavilion. Farewell dinner under the stars on your private deck. Return seaplane to Malé.

Kuredu Island Resort & Spa

14-day suggested itinerary

Amalfi Coast

Naples · Positano · Ravello · Capri · Sorrento

Days 1–2

Naples & Pompeii

Arrive Naples, private transfer. Guided private tour of Pompeii at golden hour, pizza masterclass with a Neapolitan pizzaiolo, sunset aperitivo on the waterfront.

Grand Hotel Parker's, Naples

Days 3–5

Positano

Arrive by private boat. Bougainvillea-draped streets, custom linen shopping, boat day to sea grottoes, dinner at La Sponda by candlelight with 1,000 candles lining the terrace.

Le Sirenuse, Positano

Days 6–7

Ravello & Villa Cimbrone

Private transfer to Ravello. Morning stroll through Villa Rufolo gardens, private concert in the cliff-top amphitheatre, long lunch at Rossellinis with Michelin-starred tasting menu.

Belmond Hotel Caruso, Ravello

Days 8–10

Capri

Hydrofoil to Capri. Blue Grotto at dawn, chairlift to Monte Solaro, private yacht charter around the Faraglioni rocks, Aperol spritz on the Piazzetta.

Capri Palace Jumeirah

Days 11–14

Sorrento & farewell

Limoncello distillery visit, truffle pasta cooking class, sunset on a private terrace overlooking Vesuvius, last evening gelato passeggiata through the old town.

Grand Hotel Excelsior Vittoria, Sorrento

Ready to make this your journey?

14-day suggested itinerary

The Serengeti

Arusha · Central Serengeti · Ngorongoro · Zanzibar

Days 1–2

Arusha Gateway

Arrive Kilimanjaro International, stay on a coffee farm estate. Cultural tour of Arusha town and Maasai market, briefing dinner with your private safari guide.

Legendary Lodge, Arusha

Days 3–6

Central Serengeti

Fly-in camp arrival. Twice-daily game drives in a private 4WD, tracking lion prides and cheetah at dawn. Sundowners on a kopje, bush dinners under lantern-lit acacia trees.

andBeyond Serengeti Under Canvas

Days 7–9

Great Migration

Follow the wildebeest migration with expert naturalists. Hot air balloon at sunrise over the plains, champagne landing breakfast. Private river crossing viewing at the Mara.

Singita Mara River Tented Camp

Days 10–11

Ngorongoro Crater

Descend into the world's largest intact caldera. Rare black rhino sightings, pink flamingo lakes, picnic lunch on the crater floor with views of Kilimanjaro.

andBeyond Ngorongoro Crater Lodge

Days 12–14

Zanzibar beach finale

Fly to Zanzibar. Spice farm tour in Stone Town, dhow sunset cruise, three nights of rest on powder-white beaches with turquoise Indian Ocean.

The Residence Zanzibar

Ready to make this your journey?

14-day suggested itinerary

New Zealand

Auckland · Bay of Islands · Rotorua · Queenstown · Fiordland

Days 1–2

Auckland & Waiheke Island

Arrive Auckland. Ferry to Waiheke Island for wine tasting at Stonyridge and Mudbrick, oysters at Cable Bay. Evening cocktails overlooking the Hauraki Gulf.

The Hotel Britomart, Auckland

Days 3–4

Bay of Islands

Fly north to Kerikeri. Scenic cruise of the Bay of Islands, dolphin watching, visit to Cape Reinga. Private charter option available.

Kauri Cliffs Lodge, Matauri Bay

Days 5–6

Rotorua & Geothermal Wonders

Fly to Rotorua. Champagne breakfast at Te Puia, private Maori cultural evening, heli-tour over volcanic landscape. Private Polynesian hot pools at sunset.

Treetops Lodge & Estate, Rotorua

Days 7–9

Queenstown & Lake Wakatipu

Fly south. Private jet boat on the Dart River, helicopter glacier landing, award-winning dinner at Amisfield Winery. Morning yoga with mountain views.

Eichardt's Private Hotel, Queenstown

Days 10–11

Milford Sound & Fiordland

Scenic flight into Milford Sound. Overnight luxury cruise — waterfalls, seals, dolphins, kayaking at dawn in the ancient fiord.

Milford Sound Lodge, exclusive suite

Days 12–14

Central Otago & Christchurch

Private pinot noir tastings in Gibbston Valley. Hot air balloon over Canterbury Plains at sunrise. Farewell dinner at Twenty Seven Steps, Christchurch.

The George, Christchurch

Ready to make this your journey?

14-day suggested itinerary

Japan

Tokyo · Hakone · Kyoto · Hiroshima · Osaka

Days 1–3

Tokyo

Arrive Narita, transfer to Shinjuku. Tsukiji outer market breakfast, private Shibuya crossing at dawn, rooftop omakase sushi dinner. Luxury Shinkansen day trip to Nikko.

Aman Tokyo, Otemachi

Days 4–5

Hakone & Mt Fuji

Private chartered car to Hakone. Ryokan check-in, kaiseki dinner, open-air onsen with views of Fuji at sunrise. Glass-fronted cable car over volcanic landscape.

Gora Kadan Ryokan, Hakone

Days 6–9

Kyoto

Shinkansen to Kyoto. Private temple access at Fushimi Inari and Kinkaku-ji before the crowds. Tea ceremony with a grandmaster, geisha dining experience in a private machiya.

Aman Kyoto, Kinkaku-ji district

Days 10–11

Hiroshima & Miyajima

Peace Memorial Museum private tour, ferry to Miyajima to see the floating torii gate at high tide. Matcha ceremony at a riverside teahouse.

Grand Prince Hotel Hiroshima

Days 12–14

Osaka & farewell

Dotonbori food crawl, Osaka Castle at dawn, cooking class for takoyaki and ramen. Farewell 10-course kappo dinner in a chef's private dining room.

The St. Regis Osaka

Ready to make this your journey?

14-day suggested itinerary

Namibia

Windhoek · Sossusvlei · Damaraland · Skeleton Coast · Etosha

Days 1–2

Windhoek Arrival

Arrive Windhoek, private city tour through the colonial German quarter. Sundowner cocktails on a hilltop terrace. Briefing dinner with your private safari guide.

The Olive Exclusive, Windhoek

Days 3–5

Sossusvlei & the Namib Desert

Fly to Sossusvlei. Sunrise climb of Dune 45 and Big Daddy. Walk the stark white Deadvlei pan at golden hour. Hot air balloon over the Namib, champagne landing breakfast.

Sossusvlei Desert Lodge, &Beyond

Days 6–7

Damaraland & Desert Elephants

Fly north to Damaraland. Track rare desert-adapted elephants and black rhino with expert naturalists. Visit Twyfelfontein rock engravings (UNESCO). Night drive under extraordinary skies.

Damaraland Camp, Wilderness Safaris

Days 8–9

Skeleton Coast

Fly to the haunting Skeleton Coast. Walk among Cape fur seal colonies, explore shipwrecks, witness the desert meeting the cold Atlantic.

Hoanib Skeleton Coast Camp

Days 10–12

Etosha National Park

Fly to Etosha. Game drives around the famous salt pan — lion, leopard, elephant, black rhino. Floodlit waterhole viewing from your private suite deck at night.

Onguma The Fort, Etosha East

Days 13–14

Swakopmund & Farewell

Fly to Swakopmund. Morning quad biking over dunes, afternoon oysters at the waterfront, sunset kayak with seals. Final farewell dinner and homeward transfer.

The Delight Hotel, Swakopmund

Ready to make this your journey?
